When you choose to study here, you’ll join a welcoming community of like-minded students and outstanding academics who share your passion for your subject.

Come and visit us on one of our open days. You’ll get to explore our campus and meet some of the staff who will be teaching you. You’ll also be able to chat to current students and get a feel for the University and our city. If you can’t make an open day, campus tours are available throughout the year. Booking for the autumn open days begins in June.

Finding the right course for you

Our size and international reputation, and the fact that we have more teaching and research staff than almost any other UK university, enables us to offer you one of the widest ranges of courses in the UK.
